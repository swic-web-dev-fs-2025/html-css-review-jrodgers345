Follow the winding Road to JavaJam

We're little out of the way, but take a drive in the country down Garrett Bay Road to JavaJam today! Indulge in our locally roasted free-trade coffee and home-made pastries. You'll feel right at home at JavaJam!

Friendly and eclectic — JavaJam Coffee Bar is the perfect place to take a break, enjoy a refreshing beverage, and have a snack or light meal.
